Pilaf

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Meat 900g
Rice 700g
Oil 130ml
Onion 150g
Carrot 500g
Garlic 1pc

Step 1: Roast onion and carrot on oil.
Step 2: Add meat, wait 20 minutes.
Step 3: Add a little water , boil 10 minutes.
Step 4: Add rice, add garlic, add water. Brew on slow fire 40 minutes

Olivier

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Cartoffel 7pcs
Salted cucumber 6pcs
Cooked eggs 6pcs
Onion 150g
Carrot 5pcs
Beans 1pc
Sausage 300g
Mayonnaise 200g

Step 1: Cut everything in cubes.
Step 2: Add salt and mayonnaise and mix.
